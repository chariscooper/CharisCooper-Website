Charis Cooper

Behavior Technician

I am a dedicated and experienced behavior technician with a passion for helping individuals with behavioral challenges lead fulfilling lives. With a strong educational background in psychology and a wealth of hands-on experience, I have honed my skills in behavior analysis and intervention strategies. My journey in the field of behavior technology began with a profound interest in understanding human behavior and a desire to make a positive impact on the lives of others.I am working on earning my bachelor's degree in psychology from the University of California, Riverside, where I have gained a comprehensive understanding of the principles of behaviorism and psychological theories. This educational foundation provided me with the necessary knowledge to delve deeper into the field of behavior analysis. Subsequently, I pursued specialized training and obtained certification as a Board-Certified Assistant Behavior Analyst (BCaBA). This certification equipped me with a strong theoretical framework and practical skills to assess, design, and implement effective behavior intervention programs.Over the past several years, I have had the opportunity to work in various professional settings, including clinics and community organizations.

My work as a behavior technician has involved collaboration with interdisciplinary teams to support individuals with autism spectrum disorder, developmental disabilities, and other behavioral challenges. I have been involved in conducting functional behavior assessments (FBAs) to identify the underlying causes of challenging behaviors and developing behavior intervention plans (BIPs) tailored to each individual's unique needs.One of my key strengths as a behavior technician lies in my ability to build meaningful and trusting relationships with clients and their families. I understand the importance of effective communication and collaboration in the field of behavior technology. I have facilitated parent training sessions, empowering families with the knowledge and skills necessary to reinforce positive behaviors at home. By fostering a supportive and empathetic environment, I have helped families navigate the challenges associated with behavioral issues, enabling them to advocate for their loved ones effectively.

In addition to my hands-on experience, I stay abreast of the latest research and developments in the field of behavior analysis. I regularly attend workshops, conferences, and seminars to expand my knowledge and refine my techniques. I am well-versed in evidence-based interventions, such as Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), and I leverage this knowledge to create tailored intervention strategies that promote skill acquisition and behavior modification. Beyond my technical skills, I am deeply passionate about creating inclusive environments where individuals of all abilities can thrive. I am an advocate for promoting awareness and acceptance of neurodiversity, and I actively engage in community outreach initiatives to educate others about behavioral challenges and the importance of evidence-based interventions.As a behavior technician, I am driven by a genuine desire to make a positive difference in the lives of individuals and their families. I am committed to continuous learning and professional growth, aiming to enhance my skills and expertise to better serve those in need. Through my dedication, compassion, and expertise, I strive to contribute meaningfully to the field of behavior analysis and support individuals on their journey toward a brighter and more independent future.
